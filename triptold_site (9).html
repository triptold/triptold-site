Why we built this

You do not need another creator platform.

You need a different one.

Travel decisions carry enormous emotional weight. People invest time, money, hope and memory into experiences they cannot get back.

A bad jumper is annoying. A bad holiday can become a lost anniversary, wasted savings, or precious time you cannot get back. People are not buying travel logically. They are buying anticipation, identity, connection and escape.

Much of modern travel content is driven by incentives that have little to do with genuine experience. Popularity is not the same thing as trust.

That is what we are building. Deliberately, carefully, and with the right people from the start.
